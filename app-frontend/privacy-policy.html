Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 12, 2026  |  Effective date: April 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how ARVANN ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. By using ARVANN, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. About ARVANN

ARVANN is a B2B business management application designed for manufacturers, distributors, and traders. It helps businesses manage inventory, products, quotes, accounting reports, payments, and team communication in one place.

2. Information We Collect

Account & Profile Information

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Business/company name, address, and GST details
  • Profile photo (optional)

Business Data

  • Product catalog, inventory records, and stock levels
  • Quotes, orders, and purchase/sales transactions
  • Accounting data (profit & loss, GST reports, party outstanding)
  • Business contacts (customers and suppliers)

Photos & Camera

  • Photos you take or select from your device to upload as product images
  • We only access your camera or photo library when you choose to upload an image

Communication Data

  • Messages sent within the in-app chat feature

Payment Information

  • Payments are processed by Razorpay, a third-party payment gateway. We do not store your card details or banking credentials on our servers. Please refer to Razorpay's Privacy Policy for more information.

Device & Usage Information

  • Device model, operating system version
  • App usage activity and feature interactions
  • Push notification tokens (to send you alerts)

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create and manage your ARVANN account
  • To provide and operate all app features (inventory, orders, accounting, chat)
  • To process payments and transactions
  • To send push notifications about orders, quotes, and business activity
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations (e.g. GST compliance)
  • To respond to your support requests

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information to anyone. We may share data with:

  • Razorpay – to process payments. Their privacy policy governs data they receive.
  • Cloud infrastructure providers – to host and store app data securely (e.g. AWS).
  • Law enforcement or regulators – if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of our users.

All third-party service providers are contractually required to protect your data and use it only for the services they provide to us.

5. Permissions We Request

  • Camera – Used only when you choose to take a photo for a product listing.
  • Photo Library – Used only when you choose to select an existing image to upload.
  • Push Notifications – Used to alert you about orders, quotes, messages, and business updates. You can disable notifications at any time in your device settings.
  • Internet Access – Required for all app features to function.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your data is stored on secure servers. We use industry-standard security measures including HTTPS encryption for all data transmission, and secure storage on your device for authentication tokens.

While we take reasonable steps to protect your information, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your account credentials private.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide our services. If you delete your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required to retain it by law (e.g. financial records for GST compliance).

8. Children's Privacy

ARVANN is intended for use by businesses and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will delete it promptly.

9. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of push notifications via your device settings
  • Withdraw camera or photo library permissions via your device settings

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at the email address below.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you through the app or by email. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

Continued use of the app after changes are posted means you accept the updated policy.
